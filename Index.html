Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

The process of developing and putting into practice fresh concepts or advancements that enhance value or address issues is known as innovation.

Sustainability

The goal of sustainability is to satisfy current demands without endangering the capacity of future generations to satisfy their own,In order to meet current requirements without sacrificing the capacity of future generations to meet their own, sustainability is the practice.

Infrastructure

Through smart urbanization, green energy, inclusive growth, and improved connectivity, the Bangladesh 2.0 infrastructure pillars prioritize sustainable development.

Empowerment

Innovation, inclusion, sustainability, and digital transformation are the main pillars of Bangladesh 2.0 of Empowerment, which aims to propel socioeconomic advancement.

The Vision of Future

In order to reshape the country's future, Bangladesh 2.0 seeks to embrace innovative technology, encourage equitable progress, and guarantee sustainability. With efforts in digital transformation, renewable energy, and strong infrastructure, the nation is poised to become a worldwide development model.

Digital Transformation

Establishing a digital ecosystem across the country to improve service accessibility and connectivity for all inhabitants.

Green Energy

Solar and wind energy investments to guarantee a sustainable and environmentally friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Constructing cutting-edge infrastructure to promote global competitiveness and economic growth
